Who We Are

Building a World-Class Critical Minerals Platform

Boet Materials Corp. is a North American critical minerals company developing the Clearwater Basin project in Alberta, Canada. At pre-feasibility stage with a validated resource base, proprietary processing technology, and significant government backing, the company is on a clear path to commercial production. Our sorbent-based direct lithium extraction process positions us as a low-cost, low-footprint producer in one of the world's most stable mining jurisdictions.

Scale

One of the largest-tier global resource bases, with 16.2 Mt measured & indicated and a 50+ year reserve life.

Technology

Proprietary sorbent-based direct extraction process with a fraction of the environmental footprint of conventional methods.

Jurisdiction

Alberta, Canada — one of the most stable, infrastructure-rich, and mining-friendly jurisdictions on the planet.

Proprietary Process

A Better Way to Extract

Our proprietary sorbent-based direct extraction process delivers battery-grade lithium product faster than conventional methods, using a closed-loop water system with less than 10% of the surface footprint — and zero tailings or long-term waste liabilities.

The Opportunity

Structural Demand. Constrained Supply.

The global energy transition is creating unprecedented demand for critical minerals — and the supply side can't keep up.

EV & Grid Storage Demand Surging

Global lithium demand is forecast to grow dramatically through 2035, driven by EV battery manufacturing and stationary grid-scale storage deployment worldwide.

Supply Chain Reshoring

North America and the EU are mandating domestic critical minerals sourcing, creating a strategic premium for Canadian-jurisdiction assets with secure supply chains.

Supply-Side Constraints

New projects face 7–12 year development timelines, high capex, and permitting complexity — making advanced-stage assets with government backing exceptionally scarce.
