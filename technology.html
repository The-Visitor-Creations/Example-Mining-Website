Explore Our Mine

Our fully integrated mining operation encompasses extraction, processing, tailings management, and power infrastructure — all engineered for maximum efficiency and minimal environmental impact.

4 Operational Stages
End-to-End Integration
94% Gold Recovery
01 — Extraction

Open-Pit Mining

Multi-bench open-pit operation with precision blasting and GPS-guided excavation. 180-tonne haul trucks transport ore along engineered roads to the primary crusher.

2.4M t/yr capacity
5 active benches
GPS-guided fleet
02 — Processing

Gravity & CIL Circuit

Crushed ore enters a gravity separation circuit followed by carbon-in-leach processing. The thickener recycles process water while reagent systems maintain optimal chemistry.

94% Au recovery
SAG + Ball mill
Closed-loop water
03 — Tailings

Dry-Stack TSF

Filtered tailings are stacked in engineered cells with compacted lifts. No conventional tailings dam required — significantly reducing environmental risk and water consumption.

Zero tailings dams
85% water recovery
Progressive rehab

Grid + Solar Hybrid
