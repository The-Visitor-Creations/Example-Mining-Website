About Boet Materials

Our Story in Mining

Boet Materials Corp. is a critical minerals development company committed to advancing world-class lithium projects with precision, integrity, and respect for people and the planet.

Built on Expertise. Driven by Purpose.

Founded with a clear mission to advance the global energy transition, Boet Materials Corp. has grown from a regional exploration company into a vertically integrated critical minerals platform. Our team combines decades of geological, engineering, and environmental expertise.

With assets anchored in the resource-rich Clearwater Basin and emerging targets across stable mining jurisdictions in Western Canada, we bring focused expertise with local precision. Every project begins with rigorous scientific assessment and community engagement.

We believe that responsible resource development is not just possible — it's essential. Our proprietary extraction technologies reduce environmental impact while maximizing recovery, setting a new standard for sustainable mining in the 21st century.
